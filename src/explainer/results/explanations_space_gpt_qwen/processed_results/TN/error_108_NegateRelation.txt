### Take‑away for Stakeholders  
- **Carat** is unequivocally the single most influential factor for both overall price prediction and for individual diamonds.  
- **Clarity** and **cut** add positive value when they are of higher quality (lower encoded value for clarity, higher for cut).  
- **Color** improves price when the encoded value is higher (indicating better color grades).  
- **Depth‑related dimensions** (y, depth %) tend to reduce price when too small or too large; they also interact with overall size.  
- For individual diamonds, the magnitude of the prediction error is largely attributable to the carat size; small differences in carat can swing the price by thousands of dollars.  

These insights can guide sellers, buyers, and appraisers in understanding how each attribute drives valuation and where to focus when negotiating or evaluating a diamond.
